About the Institute

Leading Engineering Education
in the Region Since 2002

Miracle Institute of Technology (MIT), Kanpur, is a premier engineering institution affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. Approved by AICTE, New Delhi, the institute has been at the forefront of technical education in Uttar Pradesh for over two decades.

With NAAC A+ accreditation, NBA accredited programs, and a strong focus on industry-oriented learning, MIT produces engineers ready to meet global challenges.

Our Mission

To provide world-class technical education that nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and ethical values. To produce competent engineers and technocrats capable of contributing to national development and meeting global challenges with confidence.

Our Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in engineering education and research, recognized globally for academic rigor, industry engagement, and societal impact. To create an ecosystem fostering entrepreneurship and lifelong learning.
