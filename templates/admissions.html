Admissions 2026-27

Begin your journey at Miracle Institute of Technology

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics from a recognized board.
  • Minimum 60% aggregate in PCM (45% for reserved categories).
  • Valid score in JEE Main / UPCET / State Entrance Examination.
  • Candidates must be physically and mentally fit for the program.

Admission Process

1
Apply Online

Fill the online application form on the AKTU/UPCET portal or institute website.

2
Counseling Registration

Register for UP State Counseling based on your JEE/UPCET rank.

3
Document Verification

Submit original documents for verification at the institute.

4
Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat.

5
Orientation & Classes

Attend the induction program and begin your academic journey.
